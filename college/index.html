Campus Facilities

GYMNASIUM

The institute focuses on the health of its students as well and thus has a well-equipped gym within the campus. Both, boys and girls has a defined time to access the gym as per their comfort and availability.

LIBRARY

The Central library is fully computerized & using Open Source Library Management System ‘KOHA’. It has around 35000 volumes with 5460 titles of books. It has a very good collection of journals in print as well as electronic resources like DELNET, Inventi, (Nalanda E-Consortium), NPTEL, NDLI etc.

SPORTS FACILITIES

Dr. Akhilesh Das Gupta sports stadium in the campus has a capacity to accommodate more than 20,000 spectators. The stadium is extensively used for outdoor games like Cricket, Football & Hockey and Athletics .

HOSTELS

Hostel facility for 2000+ Boys and 1000+ Girls respectively is available in the Campus. Each Hostel has a Recreation Room with TV & Indoor Games facility.

View Hostel Rule Book

MALL & CAFETERIA

The Student’s Mall is a beautiful double storied, sloped roof building, surrounded by lush green lawns housing Food Court, Stationery & Book Shop, Salon, Dispensary and Branded Outfit.

BANKING FACILITY

The campus has a full-fledged branch of Punjab National Bank with 24X7 ATM facility. The bank offers zero balance savings bank accounts and educational loan facility to the students.
